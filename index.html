About Me

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE), National University of Singapore, Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Rui Zhang. Prior to this, I received the B.S. degree (Hons.) in information engineering and the M.S. degree in signal and information processing from the Southeast University, Nanjing, China, in 2017 and 2020, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Chenhao Qi.

Research Interests

I have a wide range of interests in design and performance optimization for wireless communication systems. My current research focuses on

  • Millimeter wave communication

  • Intelligent reflecting surface

  • Movable antenna (MA)-enabled wireless communications

Please refer to this repository for the latest updates of MA-related papers from our group!
